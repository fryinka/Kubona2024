HOW TO ENABLE COOKIES ON YOUR BROWSER
  • In the Chrome App
    1. At the top right, tap More and then Settings

    2. Tap Site settings and then Cookies.

    3. Turn Cookies on.
  • In the Firefox App
    1. Open the Firefox menu and then tap Settings.

    2. Tap Privacy and then Cookies.

    3. Tap Enabled to turn it on.
  • In other browser apps: For instructions, check the support website for your browser.
